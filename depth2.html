Deep Depth of Field

Taken with Canon EOS R6 Mark II

Deep Depth of field

Original (left), Edited (right). This photo was taken in New Jersey, the background and all it's subjects are in focus, displaying a deep depth of field. The original photo was taken with a high aperture, as ideal for landscape photos, but the high ISO clashed with the natural sunlight already present. I edited this photo with the curve tool in photoshop, lowering the brightness in the center and lower right corner. The dimmed lighting emphasizes the detail in the cherry blossoms, making the tress and the people stand out against the sky in the far back.
